Why with us

 

Mamta Travels has a solution that exclusively meets all client transportation needs with safety, punctuality and efficiency.

We operate a fleet of over 150 vehicles and all our chauffeurs are professionally trained to provide the highest standards of service in the industry. English speaking chauffeurs are also provided on request. We conduct specialized training programs for our chauffeurs where we emphasize & train them as per the overall service standards expected by our discerning corporate & VIP clients.

Why with us

Discipline

Discipline is an important aspect of our service. All our chauffeur are trained to provide the warmth of personalized attention and consistency of service that will make you coming back to us.

Reliability is our prime concern

We believe that the chauffeur is the core of our service. Therefore special emphasis on chauffeur training is given. All our chauffeurs are well trained, polite, police verified and carry mobile phones. 

Safety and Comfort

Each driver is thoroughly interviewed & tested by the management, which ensures that you feel safe and comfortable throughout your journey. Once past the interview process, a potential chauffeur is run through a three day training program in defensive driving, etiquettes, policies and procedures of the company. 

Vehicle maintenance

Proactive vehicle maintenance is done to minimize breakdowns & avoid costly apologies. Vehicles CLEANLINESS By virtue of our experience, we understand the requirements and expectations of the discerning guest. We have a complete maintenance dept. with systems in place to ensure preventive maintenance is done at the right intervals.

Attractive prices

Vehicles are available on monthly / yearly contracts at attractive prices. Attractive discounts will be given on bulk bookings.

Dedicated Help Desk

Mamta travels provides a dedicated Help Desk, complete Route Planning & Rostering, Vehicle Health Check-up, compliance and structured MIS Reports etc., through a team of trained and experienced staff, to ensure smooth and trouble-free operations.
