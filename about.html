About

Be it for your own use, guests from out of town or a wedding in the family, you can depend on Mamta Travels to provide you a car. You name your requirement and we can meet it. Daily rentals, Airport Transfers or Long Term Rentals. We have an option attuned to your specific need. Established in 2007, we operate a fleet of over 150 vehicles. The Corporate specialist Mamta Travels is professionally managed and finely attuned to the needs of the business traveler. Mamta Travels are currently serving the ETS requirements of a no. of clients like ANI Technologies Pvt Ltd (OLA), Jet Airways, Bayer cropscience Ltd, Convergys India Services Pvt. Ltd, Lanxess India Pvt. Ltd. We have fleets comprising of Toyota Innova, Ford Ikon, Ford Fiesta, Tata Indica, Tavera, Xylo, Sumo, Tempo Travelers & Large buses. All our vehicles come equipped with Front & Rear seat belts, Stereo system, Fire Extinguisher, Torch, First Aid safety kit and Umbrella.
